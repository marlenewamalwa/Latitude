    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about working with us

    What makes Latitude Digital different from other marketing agencies?

    We combine AI-driven technology with deep cultural intelligence specific to African markets. Our proven track record shows consistent 3X growth impact, and we offer a unique blend of global best practices with local expertise. Plus, our girl-powered, Gen-Z team brings fresh, digital-native perspectives.

    What industries do you specialize in?

    We work across multiple sectors including tech startups, healthcare & digital health, tourism & hospitality, fintech, education, NGOs & development organizations, SMEs, corporates, and sector associations. Our diverse portfolio includes over 120 clients across these industries.

    How long does it typically take to see results?

    While timelines vary by project scope, most clients see measurable improvements within 6-12 months. Our case studies show 2-5X growth in lead generation, up to 4X increase in website traffic, and 25% customer acquisition cost savings through AI optimization.

    Do you work with businesses outside Kenya?

    Yes! While we're headquartered in Nairobi, we serve clients across Africa and have worked with global brands. Our digital-first approach allows us to effectively support businesses regardless of location.

    What is your pricing structure?

    We offer flexible pricing models tailored to each client's needs and budget, including project-based, retainer, and performance-based options. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

    Can you help with both strategy and execution?

    Absolutely! We're a full-service agency offering everything from strategic planning to complete execution—including content creation, web & app development, social media management, PPC campaigns, and ongoing optimization.
